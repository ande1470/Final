Fran Tarkenton

Quarterback (1967-1971, 1974-1978)

Inducted: 1986

Tarkenton was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1986 after a 18-year career with the Giants, Vikings, and Falcons. He was a seven-time Pro Bowler and was named NFL MVP in 1975. Tarkenton still holds many NFL records including most career touchdown passes and most career pass completions.

Michael Strahan

Defensive End (1993-2007)

Inducted: 2014

Strahan was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2014 after a 15-year career with the Giants. He was a seven-time Pro Bowler, a four-time first-team All-Pro selection, and was named NFL Defensive Player of the Year in 2001. Strahan holds the NFL record for most sacks in a single season with 22.5.

Sam Huff

Linebacker (1956-1963)

Inducted: 1982

Huff was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1982 after an eight-year career with the Giants and Redskins. He was a five-time Pro Bowler and was named first-team All-Pro four times. Huff was known for his hard-hitting style of play and was one of the best linebackers of his era.
